How do I restore my purchase?

Open MechKit, tap on any locked feature, then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the purchase screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Can I use MechKit offline?

Yes! MechKit works completely offline. All calculations and data are stored locally on your device.

Are the calculations accurate?

MechKit uses industry-standard formulas and verified material data. However, all calculations should be independently verified before use in critical engineering applications.

Which devices are supported?

MechKit supports iPhone and iPad running iOS 17.0 or later.
