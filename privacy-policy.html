MechKit Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

Introduction

MechKit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. MechKit is designed to work entirely offline and does not require any user registration or login.

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your device's camera, microphone, or files
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools

Data Storage

All calculations and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to any external servers.

In-App Purchases

MechKit offers optional in-app purchases processed through Apple's App Store. Payment information is handled entirely by Apple, and we do not have access to your payment details.

Third-Party Services

MechKit does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

MechKit does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
